About the Campaign

Pet-Friendly City is a heartfelt initiative by CollegeTips.in, India’s most loved student community. We're on a mission to build a future where pets aren't just tolerated—they're celebrated, protected, and truly welcomed into our urban lives.

As we launch this campaign, we're inviting students, animal lovers, and change-makers to join hands and create pet-positive spaces across cities. Whether it's advocating for pet-friendly parks, organizing adoption drives, or simply spreading awareness—every small act makes a big difference.

Together, let’s transform our cities into safe, joyful havens for every paw and tail. 💛🐾

Impact Stories

From Alley Kittens to Lifelong Commitment

Steve Pack’s journey began when he discovered two orphaned kittens near his home. With help from a local rescue, he nurtured them—and soon became a dedicated volunteer, turning one rescue into a lifelong mission.

A VIP Ride to Stardom

Ira Shankman, a volunteer and retired educator, transported a pup named Sonny all the way to the Puppy Bowl XX. His road trip shows how volunteers go above and beyond—even across state lines—to give pets special opportunities.

Fostering 165 Furry Friends

Lizel Allen has fostered over 165 kittens since 2014. Her home became a launchpad for new lives, showing how fostering creates both safety and second chances.

A Family's Foster Journey

Laura Gallagher and her daughter Isabella fostered a mama dog and her pups—turning their home into a sanctuary. Their story proves that compassion can become a family tradition.

Inspired by these stories? You can make a difference too! Whether through volunteering, fostering, or simply sharing love—every act counts.

Adopt a Pet

Every animal deserves a loving home. By adopting a pet, you're not just bringing home a furry friend— you're giving them a second chance at life. Shelters and rescue homes are full of animals who have been abandoned, lost, or born without a family. When you adopt, you help reduce the stray animal population, break the cycle of overbreeding, and gain a loyal companion who will be forever grateful.

Adopted pets are known for their resilience, affection, and ability to bond deeply with humans. Choosing adoption over buying not only saves a life but also supports a movement that values compassion over commercialism. Be a hero—open your heart and home to a pet who’s waiting just for you. 🐶🐾
